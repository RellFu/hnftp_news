Global voices: Exploring opportunities in Hainan FTP en.hainan.gov.cn | Updated: 2025-08-25 What opportunities does China offer for youth development worldwide? Join Gen Z youth from China, Brazil, Germany, Russia, Bulgaria, Spain, the United States and beyond as they explore the Hainan Free Trade Port. Under the shade of coconut palms by the beach, they share perspectives on the opportunities and prospects brought by free trade and the duty-free economy!
