Hainan continues to improve its market access system, strengthen the fundamental role of competition policies and steadfastly advance market-oriented reforms. The province ensures the decisive role of market forces in resource allocation while enhancing intellectual property protection and talent service guarantees. Hainan upholds the principle that the rule of law constitutes the optimal business environment. The province is committed to establishing a law-based business environment, adhering to the principles of equal treatment, fair competition, equal protection and shared development for all types of enterprises. Hainan is actively developing an open and inclusive international business environment, focusing on providing convenient services and ensuring safe and orderly operation.
