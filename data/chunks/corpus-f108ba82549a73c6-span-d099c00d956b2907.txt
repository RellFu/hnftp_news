[Photo by Lin Qi/China Daily] Cool remedies For residents living in subtropical and tropical regions, many still hold on to traditional remedies to cool down and dispel dampness despite the availability of air conditioning. For example, to be authentically local, visitors might drink a cup of liangcha, one of the popular cooling herbal teas. In the labyrinth of qilou lanes, I find small liangcha shops and stands. They have diverse menus that include cold and warm drinks and desserts.
