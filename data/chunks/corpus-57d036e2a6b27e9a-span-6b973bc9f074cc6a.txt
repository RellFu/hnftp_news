Coffee is another hallmark of the island's culinary scene, with plantations and drinking habits introduced by the Chinese returning from Southeast Asia. In Wanning's Xinglong town, home to many overseas Chinese who returned, several farms cultivate coffee trees. Xinglong coffee beans are typically on every tourist's souvenir list before leaving Hainan. Hainan province boasts a rich culinary scene for foodies, featuring specialties such as Hele crabs, Dongshan goat, Wenchang chicken, and zaopocu hotpot.
