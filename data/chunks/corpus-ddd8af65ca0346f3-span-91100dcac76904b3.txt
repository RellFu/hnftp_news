[Photo/IC] Just two months after launching island-wide special customs operations, Hainan province is reporting a sharp rise in international arrivals and duty-free spending during the Spring Festival holiday, early evidence of how China's free trade port policies are reshaping the island into a global tourism and consumption hub. Border inspection agencies processed more than 86,000 exit and entry trips between Feb 15 and Feb 23, a 43. 3 percent increase from the same holiday period last year, according to the Haikou General Station of Exit and Entry Frontier Inspection. Travelers from Russia, Malaysia, Indonesia, South Korea, Kazakhstan and Singapore were among the leading sources of inbound visitors.
