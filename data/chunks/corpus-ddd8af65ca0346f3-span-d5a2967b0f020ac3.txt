Beyond beaches, foreign visitors are increasingly seeking cultural and wellness experiences, reflecting the island's evolving tourism offerings. Traditional Chinese medicine health tourism has gained popularity, particularly among Russian visitors. Acupuncture and cupping services are widely available in Sanya's Dadonghai scenic area, where many clinics feature Russian-language signage. Cultural programming has also expanded. The Hainan Ocean Paradise Resort, located in Lingshui Li autonomous county, is hosting an international new year carnival from Feb 7 to March 3. "This is by no means a simple introduction to performances," said Liu Xiaoou, chairman of the resort.
