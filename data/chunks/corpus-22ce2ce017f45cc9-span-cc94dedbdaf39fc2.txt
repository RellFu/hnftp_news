Unforgettable: "Chinese Culture Captivates Me" During her first three months in Hainan, Charlotte devoted herself to learning Chinese. "I was basically in self-imposed seclusion, studying Chinese every day. Hard work pays off — after three months, I passed the HSK Level 4 exam," she said. With a solid foundation in the language, she found herself deeply captivated by traditional Chinese culture — calligraphy, Qiongju opera, and Li brocade all fascinated her endlessly. Charlotte takes cultural activities with classmates.
