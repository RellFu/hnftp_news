[Photo by Ye Longbin/For China Daily] In 2024, Hainan's GDP reached 793. 57 billion yuan ($109. 53 billion), up 3. 7 percent year-on-year, demonstrating strong resilience and vitality. The total output of agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, and fisheries in the province grew by 2. 4 percent, with steady increases in the production of vegetables, fruits, rubber, and aquatic products. The industrial added value of enterprises above the designated size in Hainan rose by 7. 4 percent while manufacturing industry increased by 9. 6 percent, becoming the main drivers of industrial growth. The added value of the service sector grew by 3.
