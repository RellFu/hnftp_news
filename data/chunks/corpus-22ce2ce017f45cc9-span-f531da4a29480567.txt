[Photo/Hainan Normal University] Over the years, from sharing stories about China on social media to promoting the country through everyday conversations with fellow international students, Charlotte has consistently tried her best to help others understand China's history and its current development. By telling her own experiences — what she has seen, heard, and learned — she strives to present an objective and authentic image of China. Inspired by her, more and more international students in Hainan have become cultural ambassadors in their own right, actively contributing to the global spread of Chinese culture.
