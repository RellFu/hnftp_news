Like Aladdin with his Magical Lamp, British visitor Michael discovers a century-old English lamp in Haikou, capital of south China's Hainan province. Intrigued, he embarks on a journey to uncover its origins and finds himself captivated in a museum of lamps, each telling tales of illumination.
