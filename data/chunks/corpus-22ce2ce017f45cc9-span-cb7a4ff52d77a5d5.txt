On the first day of Chinese New Year in 2021, the then Governor of Hainan, Feng Fei, visited Hainan Normal University to greet faculty and students. Emilienne Charlotte, a student from Cameroon, performed a Chinese song "I Love You, China", which energized the crowd. Now, Charlotte's story in China has entered its ninth year. First Impression: "China is a Great Country" Charlotte's story with China began in the summer of 2016, when she was about to graduate with her bachelor's degree and was uncertain about her future. One day, she received a call from a friend in Cameroon, who was not only familiar with China's customs and culture but also spoke Chinese. "In Cameroon, there are few opportunities to learn Chinese, and I was immediately drawn to this unfamiliar language," Charlotte said.
