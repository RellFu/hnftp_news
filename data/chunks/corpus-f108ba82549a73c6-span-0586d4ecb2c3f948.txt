The Qilou Old Street area consists of dozens of alleyways and thoroughfares in the old city center of Haikou, Hainan province, which is deemed one of the country's best-preserved areas of these arcade houses. [Photo provided to China Daily] Lin Qi finds authentic island life in the labyrinth of Haikou's old streets, capturing a leisurely travel pace. When you start to feel bored or overwhelmed by a hectic holiday itinerary, hurrying from one place to another, and just fancy spending a couple of relaxing, aimless days in a seaside resort, Haikou in Hainan province comes highly recommended. Located at the northern end of Hainan, the provincial capital faces the mainland across the Qiongzhou Strait.
