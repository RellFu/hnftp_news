Avoid brands with added sugars or artificial flavors. Easy storage tips Whole coconuts can last two to seven days at room temperature. Wrapped in cling film and stored at between 14 C and 15 C, they'll keep for about two weeks. Refrigeration keeps them fresh for up to a month. Coconut water. [Photo/VCG] Pre-opened coconuts must be refrigerated and consumed quickly. Packaged coconut water should be stored away from sunlight. Once opened, drink it within 24 hours.
