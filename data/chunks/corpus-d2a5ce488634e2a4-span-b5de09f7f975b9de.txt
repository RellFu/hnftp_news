On the evening of Feb 25, over 30 journalists from media outlets including the Financial Times (UK), the Irish Times, O Globo (Brazil), Lianhe Zaobao (Singapore), Kyodo News (Japan), and Hankook Ilbo (South Korea) kicked off the China Travelogue: Hainan FTP Experience for Global Journalists event, exploring Haikou's Qilou Historical and Cultural Quarter where they immersed themselves in the Nanyang charm and Hainan culture.
