EF accounts have gained rapid market acceptance since their launch in May 2024. Featuring integrated domestic and foreign currency management, simplified procedures, and diversified application scenarios, the accounts have seen sustained growth in both numbers and transaction volumes. Financial institutions in Hainan have actively promoted EF account services. For example, China CITIC Bank's Haikou branch has opened more than 200 EF accounts, with transaction value exceeding 84 billion yuan, while China Construction Bank's Hainan branch has opened 150 accounts and provided integrated cross-border financial services for multinational enterprises.
