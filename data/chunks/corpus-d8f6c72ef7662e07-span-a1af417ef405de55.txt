Athletes from across China compete in the 2026 China Extreme Dance Performance Competition in Sanya from Jan 14 to Jan 15. [Photo provided to chinadaily. com. cn] China has opened its 2026 extreme sports season with a national-level competition in Sanya that combines street dance and athletic performance on land and underwater stages. The two-day China Extreme Dance Performance Competition, held from Jan 14 to Jan 15 in the southern island province of Hainan, brought together competitors from more than 10 provinces and regions. According to the event's organizing committee, it's China's first national-level extreme sports event combining land and water-based dance. The competition leverages policy benefits from the Hainan Free Trade Port to boost Sanya's tourism and sports industry.
