How long does the labor-dispute arbitration commission have to decide whether to accept or reject an arbitration application? Within five days from the date it receives the arbitration application, accept the application and notify the applicant of its acceptance, if it considers that the application meets the conditions for acceptance. According to Article 53 of the Labor Dispute Mediation and Arbitration Law, arbitration of labor disputes is free of charge.
