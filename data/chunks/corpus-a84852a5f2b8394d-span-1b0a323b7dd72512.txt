r. What can I do if my temporary driving permit is lost when I travel in Hainan? If your temporary motor vehicle driving permit is lost, you shall apply for the reissuance of the permit to the traffic administration of the Hainan Public Security Department. What kind of cars can I drive in Hainan? The vehicle types allowed under a temporary motor vehicle driving permit shall correspond to those authorized by the applicant’s driver’s license. Can I drive in Hainan? Overseas drivers (including those from Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan) entering for a short-term stay are eligible to apply for a temporary motor vehicle driving permit with a validity of three months.
