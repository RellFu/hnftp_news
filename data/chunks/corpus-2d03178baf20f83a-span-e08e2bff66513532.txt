Giving a friendly wave to all his fans, the Michelin Man presides over the proceedings with a smile at the opening of the Michelin Guide Food Festival in Haitang Bay on Feb 10, 2026. [Photo provided to chinadaily. com. cn] It's all fun and action right now at the 2026 Michelin Guide Food Festival, which opened with a bang on Feb 10 and is running through to Feb 12 at Haitang Bay — located in the coastal resort city of Sanya, Hainan province. The event marks the first time the globally acclaimed foodies' bible the Michelin Guide has brought a major festival to Hainan. As such, it easily stands out as one of Sanya's highest-profile international food and culture events held to date.
