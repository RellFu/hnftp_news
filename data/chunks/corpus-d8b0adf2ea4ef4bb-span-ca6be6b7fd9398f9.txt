As temperatures soar, there's nothing quite like the slightly sweet taste of coconut water to cool you down. Coconut water. [Photo/VCG] Coconut water is the clear liquid inside green coconuts. With a composition of about 94 percent water, it serves as a super hydrating choice. Abundant with minerals like potassium, calcium and magnesium, it's perfect for recharging after exercise or a hot day outdoors. In China, coconuts have been grown for over 2,000 years, especially in Hainan province, where the warm weather helps them thrive. How to pick the best coconut water Whole coconuts: For the freshest taste, choose young, green ones. Good coconut water should look clear or slightly cloudy and smell fresh. If it smells sour or off, don't drink it.
