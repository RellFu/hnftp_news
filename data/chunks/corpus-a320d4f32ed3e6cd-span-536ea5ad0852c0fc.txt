[Photo provided to en. hainan. gov. cn] Performances were staged at Manezhnaya Square near Red Square, where the Hainan artists presented nine shows over three days, highlighting the province's ethnic traditions and tropical allure. The repertoire included the opening dance Please Come to Tianya Haijiao, the nationally listed intangible cultural heritage performance Auspicious Bamboo and Wood, and creative adaptations of Russian classics using Li ethnic musical instruments, including Moscow Nights, The Cranberries Are Blooming, and Katyusha. The traditional Li Bamboo Pole Dance was also performed, showcasing Hainan's unique cultural heritage.
