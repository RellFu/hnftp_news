The Hainan Free Trade Port's multi-functional free trade accounts (EF accounts) have achieved steady expansion and efficient operation as the port advances its development, according to the Hainan branch of the People's Bank of China. Statistics show that by the end of 2025, a total of 11 banks in Hainan had launched EF account services, with 810 accounts opened. Total transactions reached 350. 9 billion yuan (about $49. 3 billion). Account holders conducted cross-border fund transfers with 91 countries and regions, including Singapore and China's Hong Kong. As a key financial infrastructure supporting cross-border capital flows, EF accounts have gained rapid market acceptance since their launch in May 2024.
