A cultural and tourism delegation from South China's Hainan province visited Moscow and St Petersburg from Feb 21 to 25, 2026, to promote tourism and deepen cultural exchanges with Russia at the invitation of the Moscow City Tourism Committee. Actors from Hainan gave performance in Russia, Feb 25, 2026. [Photo provided to en. hainan. gov. cn] Organized by the Department of Tourism, Culture, Radio, Television and Sports of Hainan Province, the delegation included more than 10 performers from the Hainan Provincial Ethnic Song and Dance Ensemble. They participated in the 2026 Moscow Spring Festival celebrations, a major cultural event hosted by the Moscow City Government and supported by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation and the Chinese Embassy in Russia.
