Surf Paradise: Riding the waves of Hainan
Welcome to Hainan — China’s tropical surf sanctuary, where warm turquoise waves roll year-round onto sun-drenched shores, and the rhythm of the ocean sets the pace of life.This guide brings you seven of the island’s most iconic and must-visit surf spots.Explore Hainan's coasts: 10 themed routes for your perfect getaway
We have meticulously curated 10 themed sea routes of varying durations to unlock many ways of experiencing Hainan's marine tourism!Hainan's six curated tourism routes
Hainan has unveiled six curated tourism routes, covering themes including coastal leisure, rainforest exploration, ethnic culture experiences, historical culture and wellness tourism.Monkey Island
On the southeast coast of Hainan, about 14 kilometers south of Lingshui, lies the Nanwan Peninsula, which is not only the world's only tropical island macaque nature reserve, but also a secret world that combines pristine nature, Tanka culture, and tropical island vibes.Sanya: Houhai
Houhai Bay, located in Haitang Bay, Sanya, has gradually become a gathering spot for surfing enthusiasts. Houhai has integrated tourism with sports, culture, and industry, giving rise to distinctive businesses such as surfing clubs, inns, and guesthouses.West Island
West Island, the largest of the small islands off the coast of Sanya, Hainan province, has been home to fishing tribes for generations. The island's idyllic surroundings of sandy beaches, rocky coves, and an interior filled with palm trees are now the perfect vacation getaway.Haikou sunsets: Romance of the coconut city
Haikou enjoys a tropical monsoon climate. The long summer days provide excellent evening light, creating stunning sunsets that occur frequently and are highly visible thanks to the city’s excellent air quality.Ocean Flower Island: Dreamy one-stop vacation destination
Between Danzhou’s bright blue skies and deep blue seas, a unique flower has bloomed - Ocean Flower Island, composed of three man-made flower petal islands, is a haven for families, young couples, and anyone in search of a quality, fulfilling dreamy island escape.Hainan Island’s tropical rainforests teem with life
In the center of Hainan island there is a lush green carpet filled with ancient trees, drooping vines, chirping insects and singing birds... this is China’s densest, most bio-diverse, best preserved, and largest contiguous continental island tropical rainforest.Feng Xiaogang Movie Town
