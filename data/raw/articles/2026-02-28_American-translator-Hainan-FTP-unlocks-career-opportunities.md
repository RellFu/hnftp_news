The Hainan Free Trade Port stands as a testament to China's commitment to economic openness and innovation. For Marian Rosenberg, an American translator who has resided on the island for two decades, Hainan FTP's emphasis on international exchange has been particularly advantageous for the growth of her business.
"The free trade port gives Hainan more opportunities to communicate with the world and that drives demand for more Chinese to English translation, which is good for my career," she explains.
Rosenberg regards Hainan as her second hometown and Haikou, capital of Hainan, her favorite resort city. This tropical paradise, blessed with over 1,000 kilometers of coastline, hot springs, coffee beans and fresh fruit, offers a perfect blend of leisure and professional potential.
"In Haikou, you can both enjoy your life and your job. Living here, your job will keep you in contact with both China and the world at large," she says.
