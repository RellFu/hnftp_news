Hainan province is home to a growing number of world-class golf courses and high-end fitness facilities, offering residents and visitors a wide range of options for leisure, wellness and an active lifestyle.
Below is a curated list of notable golf courses and premium fitness centers across the island.
Golf courses in Hainan
Hainan's golf courses are known for their tropical landscapes, coastal settings and international-standard designs. From volcanic terrain to seaside fairways, these venues combine sport, scenery and world-class facilities.
1. Mission Hills Golf Club (Haikou)
[Photo/VCG]
The world's largest lava rock golf course complex, featuring a 27-hole, par-108 layout with a total length of 11,306 yards. The club is home to China's only 828-yard, par-6 hole and multiple lake-view fairways.
Address: No 1 Mission Hills Dadao, Longhua district, Haikou
Contact: (+86) 898 68683888
2. The Dunes at Shenzhou Peninsula Golf Club (Wanning)
[Photo/VCG]
Located in Shenzhou Peninsula Tourism Resort, this is one of Asia's premier golf courses, blending natural coastal terrain with international tournament standards. The seaside course, designed by renowned architects, features 36 holes.
Premium fitness centers in Hainan
Hainan also offers a selection of high-end fitness centers, many of which are located within luxury hotels and resorts, providing professional facilities and a refined workout environment.
1. Fitness Center, Rosewood Sanya
[Photo/Rosewood Sanya]
A high-end seaside fitness center equipped with advanced facilities, offering guests a premium workout experience in a luxury resort environment.
Address: No 6 Haitang Beilu, Haitang district, Sanya
Contact: (+86) 898 88716666
2. Fitness Center, Haikou Marriott Hotel
[Photo/Marriott Bonvoy]
Featuring five-star hotel facilities, the center also offers private training services for guests seeking a personalized fitness experience.
Address: No 292 Binhai Dadao, Xiuying district, Haikou
Contact: (+86) 898 6870 8666
3. Fitness Center, The Sanya EDITION
[Photo/The Sanya EDITION]
Featuring state-of-the-art fitness equipment, this center offers an excellent environment for fitness enthusiasts, making it ideal for guests who wish to enjoy high-quality workouts while on vacation.

Contact: (+86) 898 8888 9999
